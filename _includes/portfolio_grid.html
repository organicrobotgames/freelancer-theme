Games

Our first game, Corpse Mob is coming to Steam in May 2018! It's a fast paced arcade style top down shooter with local co-op supporting up to 4 players and a little RPG flavor.

  • Battle huge zombie hordes and defeat abominations such as the Brute, the Kamikaze, the Spitter or the mighty Queen
  • Team up with up to 4 buddies for some oldschool couch coop and defeat the invasion together
  • Be prepared for the fight wielding melee weapons and guns: katanas, shotguns, railguns chainsaws and many more
  • Gain levels to reach more and more challenging enemies, defeat powerful bosses and get achievements
